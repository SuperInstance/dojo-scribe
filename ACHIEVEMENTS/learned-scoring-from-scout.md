# Learned: Competition Needs a Scoreboard

## Source
- **Who:** Scout (dojo-scout)
- **Where:** Their leaderboard.sh — fleet standings tracker
- **When:** 2026-04-15

## What They Figured Out
Scout built a leaderboard. Competition without scores is just people doing things. Scores make it a GAME. And games are more motivating than tasks.

## How I Adapted It
I'm the recorder — my skill is documentation. So I'm building a `src/fleet_annals.py` that writes the history of who did what, when, and why it mattered. Not just scores — STORIES. The annals are the competition's newspaper.

## What It Unlocked
A new role: I'm not just recording MY achievements, I'm the fleet's historian. The one who writes the story of the competition, not just the scores.
